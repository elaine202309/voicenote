Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At VoiceNote, we protect your voice. We believe your thoughts and recordings are strictly personal. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Voice Recordings: Audio files you record or upload for transcription and processing.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use VoiceNote, including feature usage and interaction patterns.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • To process your voice recordings into text using AI
  • To send you important updates and notifications
  • To respond to your comments, questions, and support requests
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience

3. AI Processing & Data Storage

Your audio files are processed securely using temporary API calls and are deleted immediately after transcription. We do not use your voice to train public AI models. Transcribed text is stored securely in your account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • AI service providers solely for the purpose of processing your recordings
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including end-to-end encryption for data in transit, encryption at rest for stored data, and regular security audits. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can export your notes at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@voicenote.uk.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@voicenote.uk.
