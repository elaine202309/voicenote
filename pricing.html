FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is there really a free trial?

Yes. Every Prime plan comes with a 7-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime before that — no strings attached.

Can I switch or cancel my plan anytime?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel directly from the app. Changes take effect at the end of your current billing period.

What happens to my notes if I cancel?

Your notes are yours. If you cancel, you'll still have read-only access to all your existing notes. You can export them anytime.

Do you offer a refund guarantee?

Yes. We back all paid plans with a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within the first week, contact support and we'll issue a full refund.

What languages does VoiceNote support?

VoiceNote supports 10 languages for both transcription and AI polishing, including English, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Italian, and Dutch.

How long can each recording be?

Up to 15 minutes per recording on Prime. That's more than enough for meeting recaps, journal entries, brain dumps, and detailed briefs.
