FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time directly through the app store.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Our Pro and Prime plans come with a 7-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime before that.

What happens to my notes if I cancel?

Your notes belong to you. If you cancel, you will still be able to access, read, and export all your existing notes on the Free plan.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we back our paid plans with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, just contact our support.

Can I use one account on multiple devices?

Absolutely. Sign in on your iPhone, iPad, or any iOS device — all your notes sync seamlessly across every device.
