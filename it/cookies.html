Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how VoiceNote uses them, and your choices regarding cookies.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, logins, and other settings to improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

VoiceNote uses cookies for the following purposes:

CategoryPurposeDuration
EssentialRequired for the website to function — session management, authentication, securitySession
PreferencesRemember your settings such as language and theme preferences1 year
AnalyticsHelp us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it2 years
FunctionalEnable additional features like audio recording and playbackSession

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services we use:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (functional cookies)
  • Cloudflare: Security and performance (essential cookies)

Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Clear cookies when you close the browser

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of VoiceNote.

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner asking for your consent to non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time through your browser settings or by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about our Cookie Policy, contact us at support@voicenote.uk.
